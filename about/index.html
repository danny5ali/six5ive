About

six5ive studios is an avant-garde audio-visual production house.

We merge narrative cinema with music and brand storytelling — creating works that feel timeless and culturally resonant. Our projects blend film direction, original music, and visual art into immersive experiences.

Founded by Danny Ali in San Francisco, six5ive delivers films, visual albums, branded content, and campaign films that live beyond the scroll.

Recent works include:

  • “CRASH” — visual single / cult classic video
  • “RIPPIN THE SEAMS” — visual album project
  • Campaign development for Topo Chico, Neutrogena

We believe great content should feel like a film — even when it sells a product.
